A survey of methods for time series change point detection

2021-01-19

An review of change point topic; Heart Rate; Image analysis; Human activity;

Change point detection (CPD) 和 change point estimation不同？？

change point estimation tries to model and interpret known changes in time series rather than identifying that a change has occurred. The focus of change point estimates is to describe the nature and degree of the known change.

本文专注CPD，也就是只关注变点。

Medical condition monitoring
Continuous monitoring of patient health involves trend detection in physiological variables such as heart rate, electroencephalogram (EEG)(脑电图), and electrocardiogram (ECG)(心电图) in order to perform automated, real-time monitoring. Research studies investigate change point detection for specific medical issues such as sleep problems, epilepsy, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) interpretation, and understanding of brain activities.

感觉这个挺有趣的欸。特别是脑电图.

Climate change detection; Speech recognition 不感兴趣...

Image analysis
Researchers and practitioners collect image data over time, or video data, for video-based surveillance. The detection of abrupt events, such as security breaches, can be formulated as a change point problem. Here, the observation at each time point is the digital encoding of an image.
这个感觉也很有意思。
